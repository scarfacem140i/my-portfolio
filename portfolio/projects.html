Cryptocurrency

SRTCoin

Introducing SRTCoin, a new decentralized cryptocurrency built on a cutting-edge blockchain technology. SRTCoin utilizes a unique multi-layered architecture that allows for faster transaction speeds and increased scalability. SRTCoin also incorporates a built-in governance system that allows for community members to propose and vote on protocol updates, further decentralizing the project.

Inspired by the car company Dodge and its line up of SRT® (Street and Racing Technology) vehicles, the coin is known for its stability, reliability and speed.

Currently at a market cap of $22 billion.
